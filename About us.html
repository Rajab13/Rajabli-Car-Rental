About company:

Rajabli Car Rental - is one of the largest car rental companies in Ukraine. The company has been renting cars for 13 years. We have a lot of experience and desire to satisfy every client. Today our auto parc has a huge selection of cars from different classes. So Rajabli Car Rental has cars, ranging from economy class, such as Lada Priora, to premium sedans like Rolls-Royce Ghost, which you can order with or without a driver. Our company provides a full range of car rental services with a European level of service quality and European standards.

  • In our auto parc you can find cars even of the last year of manufacture;

  • Technical support 24/7. Whatever happens on the road, whether it's a puncture or a dead battery, our specialists are always ready to solve any technical issues related to the car;
  • Fully equipped and disinfected cars that undergo regular maintenance;

  • Possibility of long-term car rental for business.
